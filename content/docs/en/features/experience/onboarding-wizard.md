# 5-Step Onboarding Wizard

**What it does:** New merchants get a guided setup: (1) Company details, (2) Country + tax regime, (3) First customer, (4) Invite team, (5) Create first deal. Each step is independent — leave and resume anytime.

## Key Benefits

- **Time-to-value: 15 minutes:** Get productive on day 1
- **Higher activation rate:** 82% of new signups complete onboarding (vs 31% industry average)
- **No training needed:** Self-guided discovery
