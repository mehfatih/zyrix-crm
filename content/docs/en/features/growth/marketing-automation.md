# Marketing Automation

**What it does:** Create multi-step email and WhatsApp campaigns that trigger automatically based on customer behavior. Drip campaigns, abandoned cart recovery, birthday messages, re-engagement sequences — all without a marketer writing code.

## Key Benefits

- **Work while you sleep:** Campaigns run 24/7 automatically
- **Personalization at scale:** Each customer gets relevant content
- **WhatsApp native:** Leverage the preferred channel in MENA (88% engagement rate)
- **A/B testing:** Continuously improve conversion

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Jeddah Fashion Boutique"**

A boutique with 8,000 customers in its database was sending one monthly newsletter. Open rate: 12%. Conversion: negligible.

With Zyrix Marketing Automation, they set up behavior-based flows: abandoned cart (SMS + WhatsApp after 2 hours, then 24 hours), new customer welcome series (5 emails over 3 weeks), birthday discount (automated), win-back campaign for inactive customers (3-touch sequence). **Email revenue increased from $800/month to $14,500/month in 90 days.**

> **Sales Pitch**

> "You have thousands of customers in your database doing nothing. Each one is a potential repeat sale you're ignoring. Zyrix Marketing Automation turns that database into a revenue engine that works 24/7 without hiring a marketer."
