# Customer Loyalty Program

**What it does:** Build a complete loyalty program with points, tiers (bronze/silver/gold), rewards, referral bonuses, and birthday perks. Automatically track and redeem. Customers access their loyalty dashboard through a self-service portal.

## Key Benefits

- **Increase repeat purchases:** Loyalty members buy 67% more than non-members on average
- **Build community:** Tiered programs create aspirational status
- **Reduce acquisition costs:** Referral bonuses create viral growth
- **Data-rich:** Know which rewards drive behavior

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Beyoglu Coffee Chain" (Istanbul)**

A specialty coffee chain with 14 locations wanted to compete with Starbucks. They launched Zyrix Loyalty: 1 point per lira spent, free drink at 100 points, gold tier at 1000 points with 15% discount on all items.

Within 6 months: **Repeat customer rate rose from 28% to 54%. Average ticket size increased 23% (people added items to reach point thresholds). 3,400 new customers came through referral codes from existing members.**

> **Sales Pitch**

> "Your best customers deserve recognition. A loyalty program isn't just points — it's a psychological contract. Customers who feel valued spend 67% more and refer 3x more friends. Zyrix Loyalty is included free. Why aren't you using it?"
