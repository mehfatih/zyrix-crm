# IP Allowlisting

**What it does:** Restrict CRM access to specific IP addresses or ranges (CIDR notation). Lock the system to your office network, VPN, or specific employee home addresses. Any attempt from outside = blocked immediately.

## Key Benefits

- **Zero unauthorized access:** Even stolen credentials fail from outside your network
- **Compliance mandatory for:** Banking, healthcare, legal, government contractors
- **Reduced attack surface:** Script kiddies can't even reach your login page
- **Audit-friendly:** Demonstrate controls to auditors

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Dubai Financial Services Firm"**

A DFSA-regulated firm required SOC 2 Type II certification for a government contract. Auditors required IP restrictions. They were using a competitor CRM that didn't offer this.

Switching to Zyrix Enterprise with IP allowlisting achieved certification. **Contract value: $2.4M annually.** The IP allowlisting feature alone justified the switch.
