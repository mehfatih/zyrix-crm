# SCIM 2.0 Provisioning

**What it does:** Automatically sync users and groups between your identity provider (Okta, Azure AD, Google Workspace, JumpCloud) and Zyrix. When HR adds an employee, they appear in Zyrix automatically. When someone leaves, access is revoked instantly.

## Key Benefits

- **Zero manual provisioning:** Save IT hours per employee onboarding
- **Immediate offboarding:** No more forgotten accounts for ex-employees
- **Role sync:** Department change in HR → role change in CRM automatically
- **Audit-friendly:** Single source of truth for who has access to what

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "500-person Saudi Bank"**

A mid-sized bank was violating their own security policy: ex-employees still had active CRM accounts months after leaving, because IT manually deprovisioned across 20+ systems. SOC 2 audit flagged this as critical finding.

Zyrix SCIM integration with their Okta identity system: employee termination in HR → Zyrix access revoked within 60 seconds. **SOC 2 finding closed. Insider risk eliminated.**
