# Advanced Audit Log

**What it does:** Complete forensic record of every action: who viewed what, edited what, deleted what, when, from which IP. Before/after snapshots of every change. CSV and JSON export for compliance reviews.

## Key Benefits

- **Internal investigations:** "Who deleted this customer record?"
- **Compliance audits:** Demonstrate controls to external auditors
- **Insider threat detection:** Unusual patterns (bulk exports at 2am) flagged
- **Dispute resolution:** Prove what was promised to whom

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Kuwait Insurance Broker"**

A customer claimed they never authorized a policy change that cost them $40,000. Broker management needed proof. Without an audit log, they had no defense.

After Zyrix, audit logs show: "2024-03-15 14:22 — User Ahmed Ali authorized change to policy #12345 from IP 82.223.x.x — before: $200/month coverage, after: $240/month coverage." **Dispute resolved definitively. $40K saved.**
