# Team Collaboration

**What it does:** Built-in team chat, @mentions, comments on customer records, task assignments, and notifications. Context-rich discussions stay tied to customer records — not lost in Slack.

## Key Benefits

- **Context preservation:** Team discussions linked to specific deals/customers
- **Onboarding new employees:** They can read conversation history
- **No tool switching:** Stay in Zyrix, don't jump to Slack/Teams
- **Audit trail:** Know who said what, when

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Ankara Manufacturing"**

A mid-sized manufacturer had team discussions about customers scattered across WhatsApp, Slack, email, and verbal conversations. When a sales rep quit, the replacement was lost — no context.

After Zyrix Team Collaboration: all customer discussions lived on the customer record. **New-hire ramp time dropped from 10 weeks to 4 weeks.**
