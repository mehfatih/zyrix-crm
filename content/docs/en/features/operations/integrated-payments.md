# Integrated Payments

**What it does:** Accept payments via credit card, bank transfer, Apple Pay, STC Pay, Mada, and local MENA gateways. Create trackable payment links that can be sent via WhatsApp or email. Automatic reconciliation.

## Key Benefits

- **Get paid faster:** Payment link reduces average invoice payment from 30 days to 8 days
- **Multi-gateway:** Route payments through optimal gateway for lower fees
- **Automated receipts:** Customer gets instant receipt with company branding
- **MENA-native:** Support for local payment methods customers prefer

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Riyadh Design Agency"**

A design agency billed clients via wire transfer. Average payment time: 45 days. Chasing payments consumed 8 hours/week of the office manager's time.

After switching to Zyrix Integrated Payments (with Stripe + Mada + STC Pay), clients got a single clickable link. Payments cleared in 2-5 days. **Cash flow improved dramatically — they needed $80,000 less in working capital.**
