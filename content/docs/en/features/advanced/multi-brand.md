# Multi-Brand Management

**What it does:** Operate multiple brands under one Zyrix account. Each brand has its own branding, email templates, customer portal theme, invoice format. One operations team, many customer-facing identities.

## Key Benefits

- **Holding companies save:** One CRM for 5 brands instead of 5 CRMs
- **Consistent backend, varied frontend:** Team efficiency + brand clarity
- **Cross-brand analytics:** See performance across the portfolio

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Gulf Holding Group"**

A holding company operated 6 brands (restaurant chain, retail, consulting, events, logistics, e-commerce). Each used a different CRM. Zero cross-brand insight. $3,000/month in CRM fees across brands.

Consolidated to one Zyrix account with Multi-Brand. **Cost: $49/month instead of $3,000/month. Savings: $35,400/year.** Plus unified customer view across brands.
