# Advanced Analytics & Reports

**What it does:** Pivot table builder. Custom dashboards. Scheduled email digests (daily, weekly, monthly). Export to Excel, CSV, PDF. Drill-down from any chart to underlying records.

## Key Benefits

- **Self-service analytics:** No data team needed
- **Executive dashboards:** CEO sees metrics without asking
- **Scheduled reports:** Monday morning email: last week's performance
- **Drill-down:** See a metric, click to see what makes it up
