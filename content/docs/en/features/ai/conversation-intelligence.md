# Conversation Intelligence

**What it does:** AI analyzes your customer conversations (emails, WhatsApp, call transcripts) to detect sentiment, buying signals, and objections. Automatically tags conversations and alerts reps to time-sensitive opportunities.

## Key Benefits

- **Never miss a buying signal:** "When can we start?" = AI pings your rep immediately
- **Catch unhappy customers early:** Sentiment drops → CS team notified
- **Identify objection patterns:** Know the top 5 reasons deals stall
- **Coach better:** Managers see what top reps say vs bottom reps

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Turkish Export Trading Co."**

A B2B export company had hundreds of WhatsApp conversations daily. A key prospect sent "We're ready to move forward — what's your best price for 5 containers?" — which the rep saw 4 hours later (lunch break, then other meetings). Prospect bought from a competitor.

After Zyrix Conversation Intelligence, high-intent messages trigger immediate notifications. **Response time dropped from average 3.5 hours to 8 minutes. Deals closing in under 48 hours increased 55%.**
