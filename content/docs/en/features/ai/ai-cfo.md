# AI CFO

**What it does:** AI-powered financial assistant that analyzes your sales, expenses, cash flow, and receivables. Provides real-time insights in Arabic, English, or Turkish. Flags anomalies, predicts cash crises, and suggests optimization opportunities — all in plain language.

## Key Benefits

- **Financial clarity without a CFO:** Get insights an expensive CFO would provide
- **Early warning:** Predict cash shortfalls 30-60 days before they happen
- **Language that non-accountants understand:** "You're losing money on your Riyadh branch" instead of financial jargon
- **Available 24/7:** Ask questions anytime

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Amman Consulting Group"**

A 22-person consulting firm couldn't afford a full-time CFO ($150K/year in Jordan). Their managing partner reviewed finances in spreadsheets every Friday, missing trends.

AI CFO flagged that their largest client's payments had been slowing over the last 3 months (45 days average → 67 days). The system recommended tightening terms. **They acted before the client defaulted (which happened 2 months later). Potential loss avoided: $85,000.**

> **Sales Pitch**

> "A good CFO costs $150K+ per year. Most SMBs can't afford one. Zyrix AI CFO gives you 80% of the insight at 0.1% of the cost. It flagged a potential $85K default for one of our customers before their human accountant even noticed the pattern."
