# AI Architect / Builder / Report

**What it does:** Three AI modes for different needs:

- **Architect:** Describe your business in natural language; AI designs your optimal CRM setup (pipeline stages, custom fields, workflows)
- **Builder:** "Build me a 5-email follow-up sequence for new customers" — AI generates it
- **Report:** "What were my top customers by revenue last quarter?" — AI writes the query, shows the chart, summarizes findings

## Key Benefits

- **Setup in minutes, not weeks:** Architect configures everything in under 30 minutes
- **Instant reports:** Any question answered without a data analyst
- **Arabic conversation:** Talk to AI in your language
- **Continuously learning:** AI improves as it sees your data

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Kuwait Medical Clinic"**

A 14-doctor specialty clinic wanted to ask questions like "Which doctors have the most patient complaints?" and "Which insurance payers delay payments most?" Their Excel analyst took 2-3 days per report.

With AI Report, the clinic manager types the question in Arabic. Answer arrives in 15 seconds with charts and narrative. **Report cycle: 3 days → 15 seconds. Data-driven decisions went from monthly to real-time.**
