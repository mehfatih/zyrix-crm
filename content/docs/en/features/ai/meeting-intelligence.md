# Meeting Intelligence

**What it does:** Connect to Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams. Zyrix transcribes meetings, summarizes key points, extracts action items, logs them to the right customer record, and notifies assignees.

## Key Benefits

- **No more forgotten commitments:** Every "I'll send that by Tuesday" is captured
- **Perfect recall:** Search any word spoken in any meeting
- **Free up 30 min/meeting:** No more manual notes
- **Arabic transcription:** Supports major Arabic dialects

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Doha Professional Services"**

A 30-person consulting firm held 150+ client meetings/month across Arabic, English. Each consultant took 30-45 minutes post-meeting writing notes and updating the CRM. That's 90+ hours/month of admin.

With Meeting Intelligence, meetings auto-transcribe, auto-summarize, auto-create CRM tasks. **Admin time dropped from 90 hours/month to 8 hours/month of review. Recovered time: 82 hours/month = 1 FTE equivalent.**

> **Sales Pitch**

> "How many client commitments has your team forgotten this month? Each one is a trust erosion. Meeting Intelligence captures every promise, every action item, every decision — automatically. Your team's memory, upgraded."
