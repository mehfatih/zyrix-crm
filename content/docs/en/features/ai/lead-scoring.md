# Predictive Lead Scoring

**What it does:** AI analyzes every lead in your database and assigns a 0-100 score based on likelihood to convert. Uses hundreds of signals: behavior, company size, industry, engagement patterns, historical similar leads.

## Key Benefits

- **Focus on winnable deals:** Don't waste time on low-probability leads
- **Faster sales cycle:** Prioritize hot leads, close 40% faster
- **Better pipeline forecasts:** Scores feed accurate projections
- **Self-improving:** Model gets smarter with each closed/lost deal

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Riyadh B2B Services"**

A B2B services firm had 12,000 leads in their database with zero way to prioritize. Sales reps worked chronologically — sometimes the oldest lead got the most attention when it should have been the newest hot one.

Zyrix Lead Scoring identified the 340 highest-probability leads (7-10x conversion likelihood). Sales focused there first. **Close rate improved from 4.2% to 11.8%.** Same team, same hours — just better prioritization.

> **Sales Pitch**

> "Your salespeople work every lead with equal effort, but 80% of your revenue comes from 20% of leads. Which are they? Zyrix tells you. Result: close rates 2-3x higher with the same team."
