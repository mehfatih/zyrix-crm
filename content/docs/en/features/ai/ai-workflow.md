# AI Workflow Engine

**What it does:** Build custom automation workflows where AI performs steps. Example: When a new lead comes in → AI analyzes their website → AI drafts personalized outreach → AI schedules follow-up → AI summarizes for the sales rep. You design the flow visually; AI does the work.

## Key Benefits

- **Scale the work of 10 people with 1:** AI handles repetitive analysis
- **No coding needed:** Drag-and-drop workflow builder
- **Arabic-aware AI:** Works natively with Arabic content
- **Templates included:** "Lead Nurture," "Deal Risk Assessment," "Post-Purchase Follow-up"

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Dubai Logistics Group"**

A freight forwarding company received 300+ quote requests per week. Each required 20 minutes of research (customer's industry, cargo type, route, competition). That's 100 hours/week of admin work before sales could even call.

Zyrix AI Workflow automates research: when a request comes in, AI looks up the company website, identifies industry, checks historical rates for the route, drafts a competitive quote. **Sales rep gets a pre-researched lead ready for a 2-minute closing call. Time per lead dropped from 22 minutes to 3.**

> **Sales Pitch**

> "Your team spends 70% of their time on admin and 30% selling. Zyrix AI Workflows flip that. AI handles the repetitive research, drafting, and routing. Your people focus on conversations that matter. Typical productivity gain: 3-5x."
