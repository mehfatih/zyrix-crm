# Contract Management

**What it does:** Store, send, track, and manage all your contracts in one place. Get alerts before contracts expire. Track amendments. Maintain a complete audit trail of every signature, change, and renewal.

## Key Benefits

- **Never miss a renewal:** Automatic alerts 30/60/90 days before expiry
- **Version control:** See every amendment and who approved it
- **Searchable archive:** Find any contract clause in seconds
- **Compliance ready:** Complete audit trail for regulatory reviews

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Levana Cosmetics"**

Levana manages 47 active distributor contracts across Saudi Arabia, Iraq, and Turkey. Before Zyrix, contract renewals were tracked in an Excel sheet that got out of date. They missed renewing a key distributor contract in Iraq, losing that territory for 3 months. **Estimated loss: $45,000 in revenue.**

With Zyrix Contract Management, the system now sends automated alerts to the legal team 90, 60, and 30 days before each contract expires. No more missed renewals.

> **Sales Pitch**

> "How much revenue do you lose each year from contracts that quietly expired because someone forgot to renew? One missed distributor contract can cost tens of thousands. Zyrix Contract Management prevents that entirely — the system remembers, even when your team forgets."
