# Commission Engine

**What it does:** Automatically calculate sales commissions based on your custom rules. Support tiered structures, team overrides, clawbacks on cancellations, and multi-currency payouts.

## Key Benefits

- **Eliminate commission disputes:** Every calculation is transparent and auditable
- **Motivate your team:** Real-time dashboards show earnings as they happen
- **Flexible rules:** Tier by volume, product category, region, or customer type
- **Clawback protection:** Automatic reversal if a customer cancels within the guarantee period

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Istanbul Real Estate Group"**

A real estate firm with 28 agents was spending 3 full days each month manually calculating commissions in Excel. Disputes arose monthly because agents disagreed with management's numbers. Trust eroded.

After Zyrix, each agent sees their live commission dashboard in Arabic or Turkish. Commission disputes dropped to zero. Calculation time dropped from 24 hours to zero (fully automatic). **Agent retention improved by 22%** because agents felt fairly treated.
