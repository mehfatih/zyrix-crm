# Native E-Signature

**What it does:** Sign contracts and documents digitally within Zyrix. Fully compliant with eIDAS (European regulation), Saudi Digital Signature Law, and UAE Electronic Transactions Law. No need for external services like DocuSign or Adobe Sign ($15-45/user/month each).

## Key Benefits

- **Legal validity:** Recognized in Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar, Oman, Turkey, EU
- **Cost savings:** Replace $20/user/month DocuSign subscription with built-in feature
- **Audit trail:** Every signature cryptographically timestamped and tied to IP + device
- **Mobile-first:** Customers can sign from any phone in 30 seconds

A real estate developer was paying $24,000/year for DocuSign Enterprise for their 40 sales agents. They also had delays when international buyers needed to sign purchase agreements — printing, scanning, emailing PDFs across timezones.

With Zyrix Native E-Signature, they eliminated the DocuSign expense entirely and accelerated international deal closings from 5-7 days to under 24 hours. **Annual savings: $24,000. Deal velocity improvement: 6x.**
