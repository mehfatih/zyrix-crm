# Territory Management

**What it does:** Divide your market into geographic or segment-based territories. Assign reps to specific regions, product categories, or customer types. Automatically route new leads to the right person based on your rules.

## Key Benefits

- **No more territory conflicts:** Clear ownership eliminates internal fights over leads
- **Balanced workloads:** System can rebalance when one rep gets overloaded
- **Coverage analytics:** See which territories are underperforming
- **Multi-dimensional:** Slice by geography AND industry AND company size simultaneously

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Gulf Insurance Brokers"**

A Kuwait-based insurance brokerage had 15 agents competing for the same leads. A new website inquiry would trigger 5 agents calling the same prospect within minutes, creating a terrible customer experience and internal chaos.

Zyrix Territory Management assigned territories by (1) Emirate, (2) insurance type (life/health/motor/commercial), (3) company size. Every lead now goes to exactly one agent, automatically. **Customer complaints about "multiple agents calling" dropped 94%.** Internal conflict disappeared.
