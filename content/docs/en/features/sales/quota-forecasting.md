# Quota & Sales Forecasting

**What it does:** Set sales targets at individual, team, or company level. Track progress in real-time. Use AI-powered forecasting to predict end-of-quarter results based on current pipeline velocity and historical conversion rates.

## Key Benefits

- **No more end-of-quarter surprises:** Know 8 weeks out whether you'll hit target
- **Early intervention:** Spot underperforming reps before it's too late
- **Better investor/board communication:** Show data-backed forecasts, not gut feelings
- **Team motivation:** Gamified dashboards showing progress vs quota

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Riyadh Tech Solutions"**

A B2B software company was consistently missing its quarterly targets by 12-18%. The CFO couldn't get accurate forecasts until the last week of each quarter — by then, too late to pivot.

With Zyrix Forecasting, the AI analyzes pipeline velocity and alerts management when Q-end projection drops below 95% of target. Early warnings gave the team 6+ weeks to pivot. **Quota attainment improved from 82% to 103% in two quarters.**

> **Sales Pitch**

> "When did you last hit your sales target? If the answer is 'not this quarter,' it's because you found out too late. Zyrix predicts your quarter-end results 8 weeks early, giving your team time to fix problems instead of just reporting them."
