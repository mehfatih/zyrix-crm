# Customer Health Score

**What it does:** AI analyzes customer behavior (login frequency, support tickets, payment delays, engagement) and assigns a 0-100 health score. Automatically flags at-risk accounts before they churn.

## Key Benefits

- **Proactive retention:** Save customers before they leave, not after
- **Reduced churn:** Typical 25-40% reduction in customer loss
- **Upsell opportunities:** "Healthy" accounts are ripe for expansion
- **Focus your CS team:** Prioritize the 20% of accounts that need attention

## Real-World Example

**Real-World Example: "Cairo SaaS Hub"**

A B2B SaaS company had 12% monthly churn — they would lose ~60 customers per month out of 500. Most churn was "surprise churn" — customers quietly stopped using the product, then canceled.

After activating Health Scores, the CS team got a daily list of accounts with scores below 40. They proactively reached out. **Churn dropped from 12% to 6.5% in 4 months. That's 30 additional retained customers per month, worth ~$15,000 MRR.**

> "For every customer you lose to churn, it costs 5-7x more to acquire a replacement. Zyrix Health Scores catch silent churners weeks before they cancel. Typical clients reduce churn by 30-40% within a quarter. What's that worth to your business?"
