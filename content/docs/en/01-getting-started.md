# Getting Started

Create your account, invite your team, and import your customer list — most teams finish in under 10 minutes.

1. Sign up at [crm.zyrix.co](https://crm.zyrix.co/en/signup).
2. Invite up to 5 teammates on the Free plan.
3. Import customers from CSV or Excel.
4. Connect WhatsApp Business so incoming messages flow straight into your CRM.
5. Open the onboarding wizard for a 5-step guided setup.

You're ready to run your first pipeline.
