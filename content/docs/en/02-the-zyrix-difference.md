# The Zyrix Difference

Most CRMs were built for English-speaking markets and then translated. Zyrix was built Arabic-first, WhatsApp-first, and AI-first from day one.

- **Arabic dialect AI** — understands Gulf, Levantine, Egyptian, and Maghrebi dialects, not just Modern Standard Arabic.
- **WhatsApp-native** — every customer conversation already happens there; Zyrix makes it part of the CRM.
- **Pricing per company, not per seat** — add every teammate, every intern, every founder to one subscription.
- **Zyrix Pay + FinSuite integration** — charge customers and see the money on the same screen.
- **Built for MENA & Turkey taxes** — ZATCA, e-Fatura, VAT and KDV out of the box.
